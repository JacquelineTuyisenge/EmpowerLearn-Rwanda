Welcome to
EmpowerLearn-Rwanda

A web application designed to empower Rwandan youths,
with a focus on girls and young women, by providing a wide range of non-formal educational resources and other opportunities,
to support their personal and professional development.









Pillars of EmpowerLearn-Rwanda
Educational Empowerment

"EmpowerLearnRwanda" strives to provide accessible and engaging non-formal education on various topics, including life skills, gender equality, technical skills like HTML and CSS, and addressing social issues like gender-based violence and substance abuse.

Gender Inclusivity

One of its core objectives is to promote gender inclusivity by empowering young women with knowledge and skills, thus contributing to a more equitable society.

Community and Social Impact

By addressing social issues and promoting education, "EmpowerLearnRwanda" aligns with broader community development goals and contributes to positive social change.

About Us

Our story
I am Jacqueline, driven by my passion for preventing crime through education. I firmly believe that education empowers individuals to make informed decisions and engage in productive activities without room for criminal activities Now, EmpowerLearnRwanda is a dedicated web app on a mission. We aim to empower Rwandan youths, especially girls, through a variety of non-formal educational resources. Our focus includes essential life skills, gender equality, and technical skills like HTML and CSS. We also address critical issues like gender-based violence and related issues and we believe they can all be solved through The journey of educating Rwandan youth through this web app. EmpowerLearn-RWANDA.
Mission
Our mission is to empower Rwandan youth, particularly young women, through improved social and technical skills By creating a supportive environment that addresses unique challenges. Through educating self-awareness for their growth and success, I aim to foster an inclusive society where they can thrive and improve their living standards.
Our goals
  • To provide accessible and engaging non-formal education on various topics

  • To empower young women with knowledge and skills

  • To promote gender inclusivity

  • To address social issues

  • To contribute to positive social change

  • To align with broader community development goals
