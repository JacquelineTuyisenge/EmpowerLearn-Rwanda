Welcome to EmpowerLearn-Rwanda

a web application designed to empower Rwandan youths,
with a focus on girls and young women, by providing a wide range of non-formal educational resources and other opportunities,
to support their personal and professional development.



Pillars of EmpowerLearn-Rwanda
Educational Empowerment

"EmpowerLearnRwanda" strives to provide accessible and engaging non-formal education on various topics, including life skills, gender equality, technical skills like HTML and CSS, and addressing social issues like gender-based violence and substance abuse.

Gender Inclusivity

One of its core objectives is to promote gender inclusivity by empowering young women with knowledge and skills, thus contributing to a more equitable society.

Community and Social Impact

By addressing social issues and promoting education, "EmpowerLearnRwanda" aligns with broader community development goals and contributes to positive social change.
